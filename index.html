Join the new generation of leaders

Brooklyn Technical High School Science and Technology Program is designed to develop the next generation of STEM leaders by opening the world of science and technology to middle school students and preparing them for the challenges facing them in high school and college.

Our Objective

Every other Saturday during this school year, the students are meeting for more engineering instruction as well as test preparation of the Specialized High School Admissions Exam. Ultimately, when they arrive at Tech with their book bags stuffed and as 9th graders, the hope is for them to stay together for their first introductory course in engineering.

Their Passion

It looked like an ordinary day at the high school — except it was Saturday and all the students were in the seventh grade. Their minds swirling with solutions to engineering riddles such as creating an object from everyday materials that has neutral buoyancy; neither sinking nor floating, but hanging in the balance in water.

Your Future

And with more than 80% of the jobs in the next decade requiring engineering skills, we have the opportunity to create the Future and decide what it's like. We are not looking for the next Mark Zuckerbergs or Hedy Lamarrs. We are looking for the ones that stand out and have the passion. Because from there, we often find ourselves educating the CEOs of our Future.
